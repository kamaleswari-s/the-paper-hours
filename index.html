a letter before you go in

Hi, I'm Kamalee. I built this for you.

I want to tell you why, but slowly, if that's alright.

There was a stretch of time that was harder than most. I don't need to explain all of it here. What I remember most is wanting somewhere quiet to put things down. Nowhere to win, nowhere to fail, nowhere that asked anything of me. Just somewhere soft.

So I started building one.

It took longer than I expected, and it changed more than I expected too. There were things I built with a lot of care that just didn't end up being right, and I had to let them go, even after all that work. That part was harder than the building itself. But I think it's part of what this whole thing became about, in the end. That it's alright to undo something, even something you tried hard at, if it isn't quite what you meant.

What's left, after all of that, is what you're about to see. A place to let go of what's weighing on you. A place to say the thing you never got to say to someone. A small hope, folded into something. A tiny win, allowed to be small. A message you seal up for a version of yourself that hasn't arrived yet.

There were good moments too, quiet ones. A thing finally working the way I'd pictured it. That kind of small win, the same kind this whole site is built to hold.

I hope it's useful to you the way I needed it to be useful to me. Fold something in. Let something go. Come back to it whenever you need to.

Yours, folded and refolded,

Kamalee ❤️
